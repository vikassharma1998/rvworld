About

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California, that designs, develops, and sells consumer electronics, computer software, and online services. It is considered one of the Big Tech technology companies, alongside Amazon, Google, Microsoft and Facebook.

Apple claims that they have sold 700 million units of iPhones till date. The current global population is pegged at about 7.4 billion. So, around 9.4% of the worlds population uses an iPhone. However, only 4.77 billion of the total global population uses cell phones, out of which only 2.1 billion use a smartphone. Under these circumstances, 33.33% of the smartphone users are iPhone users

An intuitive, familiar interface.

Apple products are intuitive and easy to use, which means they’re also easy for your business to support. Our interface allows employees to use familiar actions like swiping, scrolling and tapping to collaborate, stay organised and adapt to new programmes and procedures more quickly. And each iPhone, iPad and Mac comes with powerful assistive features for vision, hearing and motor skills, so they’re accessible to everyone.

Secure by design.

Just as we design our products to be simple, intuitive, and capable, we design them to be secure. iOS and macOS are built on a UNIX foundation, making them both stable and robust. We constantly encrypt information at the system level, at rest and in transit, to protect corporate data. Regular software updates protect iOS and macOS from emerging security threats. Face ID and Touch ID use your face or your fingerprint to unlock your device and authenticate apps. And we have safeguards in place to ensure that both third-party and in-house apps can only access data in authorized ways.
